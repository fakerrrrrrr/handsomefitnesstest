Membership FAQs

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, bank transfer, and credit cards. Payment is required at the time of registration.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes, monthly and long-term memberships can be frozen for up to 30 days with advance notice.

Is there a joining fee?

No joining fee required. The price you see is the price you pay.

Can I bring a guest?

Monthly members receive 1 guest pass per month. Long-term members can bring a friend anytime.

What if I want to cancel?

Day passes are non-refundable. Monthly memberships can be cancelled with 7 days notice. 6-month memberships are non-refundable.

Do you offer student or senior discounts?

Please contact us directly to inquire about special rates and promotions.
